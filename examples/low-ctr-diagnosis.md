# Why Low CTR Is Often a Packaging Problem

A video idea can be decent and still not earn the click.

That does not automatically mean the content is bad.

Often the problem is the package around the idea.

## Common packaging failure points

### 1. The title promise is too vague

The viewer understands the general topic, but not why this version of it is worth clicking.

### 2. The click reason is not obvious fast enough

The package does not surface a clear tension, payoff, or curiosity trigger.

### 3. The audience signal is too broad

The draft feels generally relevant, but not clearly meant for one specific viewer segment.

### 4. The thumbnail and title are not helping each other

They may repeat the same idea instead of splitting the work.

## Working framing

Low CTR is often a packaging diagnosis problem before it is a content problem.
