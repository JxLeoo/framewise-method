# Packaging Dimensions

## 1. Clarity

Can the viewer understand the topic and angle quickly?

Signals:

- the subject is obvious
- the angle is readable
- the viewer does not need to decode the package

## 2. Specificity

Does the package feel concrete enough to be believable?

Signals:

- clear scope
- clear pain point
- numbers, contrasts, or examples when appropriate

## 3. Click Motivation

Is there a clear enough reason to click now?

Signals:

- tension
- payoff
- curiosity with direction
- a visible next step or result

## 4. Audience Fit

Does the right viewer feel this package is meant for them?

Signals:

- a recognizable viewer segment
- a relevant struggle
- stronger fit than a broad generic label

## 5. Packaging Synergy

Are the title and thumbnail helping each other?

Signals:

- low redundancy
- shared core promise
- one element names the topic while the other sharpens the click reason
- low internal conflict
