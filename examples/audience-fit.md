# Audience Fit

One common mistake is thinking that naming a broad audience label is enough.

It often is not.

## Broad audience label

Examples:

- `For YouTubers`
- `For creators`
- `For students`

These labels can still feel too broad because the viewer may still ask:

- what kind of YouTuber?
- what stage?
- what exact problem is this solving?

## Stronger audience fit

Audience fit gets stronger when the package makes the right viewer feel:

- this is clearly for me
- this is my current problem
- this is not meant for everyone

## Better direction

Instead of:

`For YouTubers`

Try moving toward:

`For faceless YouTubers stuck under 1k subscribers`

That is not just a label.

It is a clearer viewer segment plus a clearer struggle.
