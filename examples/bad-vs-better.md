# Bad vs Better

This example shows the kind of packaging difference Framewise is designed to make visible.

## Same topic

Faceless YouTube channel growth.

## Bad package

Title:

`Faceless Channels Need This`

Why it is weak:

- too broad
- no clear problem
- no clear payoff
- does not signal why someone should click now

## Better package

Title:

`Why Most Faceless YouTube Channels Stay Stuck`

Why it is stronger:

- names the audience
- names the pain point
- gives a more concrete click reason

## Main lesson

Stronger packaging is usually not about sounding fancier.

It is about helping the right viewer understand, quickly:

- this is for me
- this is my problem
- this is why I should click
